Hi, my name is Tvisha and I have some advice for you!

Practice regularly!

  • Set yourself a realistic goal for how long you want to practise everyday and try your best to complete you aim
  • Don't be disheartened if you don't meet your goal. We all have good and bad days
  • Keep yourself motivated to keep coding!

Motivation

  • Work on projects and challenges that you feel have importance
  • Challenge yourself as you will feel a sense of achievement once you solve a hard problem
  • Remember to take breaks and to treat yourself!
